Our Story

About CurricuForge

Transforming curriculum design through the power of local AI inference — fast, private, and intelligent.

Our Mission

Democratizing Quality Education through AI

CurricuForge was built to solve a fundamental challenge in education: designing a structured, effective, and industry-relevant curriculum is incredibly time-consuming and expertise-heavy.

We leverage IBM's Granite 3.3 2B model running locally via Ollama to analyze educational goals and output complete, coherent syllabi tailored to individual learners, institutions, and industries.

Our platform is completely self-contained. No data ever leaves your machine, respecting both privacy and sovereignty.
