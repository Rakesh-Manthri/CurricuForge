About CurricuForge

Revolutionizing education through local, private, and intelligent curriculum design.

Our Mission

CurricuForge aims to bridge the gap between industry requirements and educational content. By leveraging state-of-the-art AI, we help educators and students create structured learning paths that matter.

The Technology

We utilize IBM Granite 3.3 2B, a powerful and efficient language model, running locally via Ollama. This ensures high-quality generation without the need for expensive cloud APIs.

Privacy First

Because the AI runs entirely on your hardware, your data and curriculum designs never leave your machine. We believe in private, secure, and accessible AI for everyone.
