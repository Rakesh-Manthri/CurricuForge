FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is CurricuForge free to use?

Yes! CurricuForge is completely free. The only requirement is downloading Ollama and the Granite model (a one-time local setup).

Does it need an internet connection?

Only for initial setup. Once the Granite model is downloaded, everything runs 100% offline on your local machine.

Can I customize the generated curriculum?

Yes! You can adjust the topic tags, learning style, semesters, and industry focus to guide AI output to your exact needs.

What formats are supported for export?

Currently PDF export is supported via ReportLab. Additional export formats (DOCX, JSON) are on the roadmap.
